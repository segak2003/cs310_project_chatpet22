Changes and Updates since Version 2.4

Registration and Authentication
* Now able to log out of account from settings page
* Now able to return to home page from settings page without using phone's built-in back button
* Username as email bug has been fixed!

Chatting with your Pet
* Now introducing age-based personalities! Warning: Teen pets are kind of mean
* Better persistence functionality of chat history

Journal Entries
* Further personalized journal entries
* Entries based on energy, hunger, and happiness levels, as well as age and species of your pet

Interacting with your Pet
* Introducing new food options! Feed your pet with your choice from 3 delicious options: fish, kibble, and pizza for your Cat, or rice, pepper, and steak for your Dragon!
* You can no longer earn more points from interactions that already have maxed out hunger, happiness, or energy bars. We noticed you sneaky users... no more cheating!
* No longer allowed to multitask - if you're sleeping, you can't do anything else! (except make journal entries and update settings

General bug Fixes and Improvements
* Bad grammar and random whitespaces
* Faster bar depletion rates... who likes waiting?!
* Incorrect redirection of screens
* Dev-only features now removed for cleanliness and clarity
